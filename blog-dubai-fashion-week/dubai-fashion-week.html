NIF Kondhwa Bandra Thane X Dubai Fashion Week

By nifkondhwa@2026 / February 3, 2026

Dubai Fashion Week is a global hub for style, innovation, and creativity, showcasing the best in fashion from around the world. This year, our students had the incredible opportunity to participate, presenting their designs on an international stage. Their collections reflected a blend of cultural heritage and contemporary trends, impressing industry professionals and fashion enthusiasts alike. Being part of such a prestigious event has been an invaluable experience, allowing them to gain exposure, network with top designers, and refine their craft. We are proud of their achievements and look forward to seeing their future contributions to the fashion industry.
