Meet With Professional Designer

Learn directly from experienced fashion designers who share industry insights, practical techniques, and real-world guidance to help students understand professional workflows, creativity, and career expectations in fashion design, interior design and design management

Mr. Vivek Gautam

Director

Mr. Vivek Gautam a former international-level badminton player is the Director of NIF-KONDHWA. He formed this institute in the year 2003 with a vision of providing good quality education to students who want to live their Pune dreams in the world of design. His main objective in this endeavor is to make NIF Kondhwa the home of revolutionary designers, with the goal of metamorphizing the field of design.

Mrs. Mamta Gautam

Centre Head

Mrs. Mamta Gautam is the Centre Head of NIF Kondhwa. Coming from a family of garment exporters, she developed a deep understanding of fabrics and fashion from an early age. With a keen eye for design and strong industry awareness, she guides students with practical insights and leadership. She holds a Post Graduate Diploma in Dietetics and is committed to nurturing creative talent while maintaining high academic standards at the institute.

Ms. Gunalaxmi Gaitonde

She has played a key role in curriculum development for MSBTE in Fashion and Textile Designing.
